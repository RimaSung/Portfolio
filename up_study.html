OVERVIEW

Up-study is an educational consultancy offering specialized insights, analyses, and guidance to individuals seeking to study abroad. In the wake of the 2019 COVID-19 pandemic, prospective students were hesitant about the potential infection risks associated with overseas education. This led to a substantial decline in customer numbers. In an effort to address this challenge and regain our previous customer base, we allocated a substantial budget towards digital advertising. Regrettably, the outcome fell short of our expectations. Predicting covid’s end are uncertain, yet we must move forward. Consequently, we've chosen to develop fresh business strategies aimed at enticing people to seek out our services.

GOALS

Incorporate the brand spirit and the core values with substance, establishing a consistent and compelling, recognizable brand image, thereby motivating users to schedule a consultation service

Optimization

Improve the Mobile Experience

Layout revamp

I have redesigned the layout to enhance readability and accessibility on the website. On the official website, various lists and tables display different types of information. We conducted a comprehensive inventory of the information, clearly delineating the hierarchical relationships between them to improve clarity. This aligns with the characteristics of the mobile experience, allowing users to smoothly scroll through complex information.

Improve the Mobile Experience

Responsive Web Design

When creating UI components for a website, the consistent experience across all sizes of devices was a priority. We eliminate the need for users to zoom in or scroll excessively by designing an RWD website. This reduces friction and frustration, leading to a smoother and more enjoyable browsing experience.

Develop the brand image

Design System

A design system includes color schemes, typography, iconography, UI patterns, and more, with the aim of enhancing the brand message in a comprehensive and distinctive manner.

Develop the brand image

Customers reviews and success case stories

Since many customers read articles about application experience for collecting information, we believe a Word-Of-Mouth marketing strategy is the best way to build a good impression on customers. Once customers share their great experience using our service, it will encourage consumer-to-consumer communication. Thus, customer reviews and success case stories are displayed on the index page to attract potential customers to our service.

Develop the brand image

Blog

To make customers find and aware of our service on the first page of search engine results, we decided to open a blog channel that provides updated information for customers. It is also friendly to the search engine to promote our website since it can: (1) meet search intent by introducing search keywords in the article. (2) encourage customers to visit other pages by adding internal links. (3) attract customers by providing more graphics, visuals, and illustrations in the article.

Enhance accessibility

Appointment Scheduling Form

The form prompts customers to provide their basic information and preferred contact office. Upon submission, the request will sent to the consultant manager for assigning customers to a consultant through the Customer Relationship Management System.

Enhance accessibility

Articles about schools and tags

According to the report, many customers would like to gather needed study abroad information on a signal site to save time. After further observation, we learned that customers often use location, degree offered, and characteristics as search keywords when collecting school information. Based on these insights, we designed the article session to meet the needs of our customers.

Moreover, customers could retrieve information by selecting tags on some pages that aid customers to recall memory and encourage them to keep interacting with the website. By doing so, 66% of the bounce rate was reduced and impacted SEO ranking.
